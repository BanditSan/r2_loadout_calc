BUILD QUERIES [BETA]
Build Filters and Queries can be used to find and GENERATE whatever build you might be after!
Please note that queries are in BETA with a limited capacity specific to tank-based searches FOR NOW, and you guys are likely to find bugs that I did not. Please report bugs or suggestions in the discord linked at the bottom of any page.
Also note that queries currently have a combo limit of 300 million. Any query that exceeds that will be forcibly aborted and the user will be encouraged to use more strict filters.

STATS
Stat Filters are used to retrieve any and all items that ever use the stats you specify here. These apply first. Enter manually, or use presets.

EXCLUSIONS & INCLUSIONS
These filters are used to forcibly exclude or include specified items from generation.
Exclusions are used when you do not want to see an item(s) from a category in your results.
Inclusions are used when you ONLY want to see an item(s) from a category in your results.
Example: you're running a Hardcore character, and you want to provide the only items you have, as the list for that category.

LOCKS
Lock Filters are used to "lock" a slot into a given item. Say a ring slot, you want ring 1 to be Seal of the Empress. Ring 1 will lock as such, and for the purposes of generating combinations, only 3 slots will iterate. The more things you can lock, the faster this will go. Locks CAN be blank, just type two hypens(--) and then hit enter.

Import Archetypes & Traits
Enter your selections into the main calculator above, then click import to store them here.
Traits alone offer beyond billions of combinations. I will NOT loop through all possible combos with 85 points to spend, because that's stupid as hell. We have way too many points, and traits are simple enough you should be able to preselect anything that would be even remotely relevant to your target build.
Once stored, they will stay the same until another import happens.
Why lock classes too? Because they provide and remove traits based on what class is selected, otherwise I'd be fine with adding them to the pool in the right conditions. Tricky task, possibly for the future.
