EXCLUSIONS & INCLUSIONS
These filters are used to forcibly exclude or include specified items from generation.
Exclusions are used when you do not want to see an item(s) from a category in your results.
Inclusions are used when you ONLY want to see an item(s) from a category in your results.
Example: you're running a Hardcore character, and you want to provide the only items you have, as the list for that category.

LOCKS
Lock Filters are used to "lock" a slot into a given item. Say a ring slot, you want ring 1 to be Seal of the Empress. Ring 1 will lock as such, and for the purposes of generating combinations, only 3 slots will iterate. The more things you can lock, the faster this will go. Locks CAN be blank, just type two hypens(--) and then hit enter.
